Autonomous Systems & Robotics

I work as a Research Engineer with Prof. Sibin Mohan at George Washington University, focusing on autonomous drones and rovers with an emphasis on secure, low-latency communication and system resilience.

Focus Areas

  • Real-time communication under packet loss and latency
  • Multi-agent coordination and telemetry
  • Security in adversarial environments

Tooling

  • ROS, PX4
  • UDP-based pipelines
  • Vicon motion capture
  • NVIDIA Jetson, Raspberry Pi

Current Research Focus

  • Detecting buffer overflow and memory-safety vulnerabilities in drone communication stacks
  • Analyzing protocol-level weaknesses in onboard and ground-station interfaces
  • Real-time detection and mitigation of adversarial behavior

Goal: improve the safety, robustness, and reliability of autonomous systems operating in hostile or unpredictable environments.
